Game Features & Mechanics

Sort the Court stands out with its unique blend of resource management and narrative storytelling. As the ruler, you'll manage three key resources: population, happiness, and wealth. Each decision impacts these metrics differently, requiring strategic thinking and careful consideration.

The game presents various characters who approach your throne with requests, proposals, and dilemmas. Your responses shape not only the immediate outcome but also influence future events and the overall direction of your kingdom's development.

With multiple endings and countless decision paths, Sort the Court offers excellent replayability. Each playthrough reveals new scenarios and outcomes, making it an ideal game to return to during multiple coffee breaks throughout your day.
